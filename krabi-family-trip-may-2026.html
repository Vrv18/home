What 50–55% rain actually means: Not half the day in rain. Typically 8 hours of sunshine, then a 1–2 hour thunderstorm in the afternoon (3–5pm). Mornings are almost always clear. Plan outdoor activities for 7am–1pm, pool time during the rain burst, evenings are pleasant again. Sea temperature is a bath-warm 30°C.

The route

Fly Bangalore → Kuala Lumpur → Krabi (AirAsia, total ~6–7h with layover). Visa on arrival at Krabi airport (2,000 THB per adult, free for children under 15). Stay in Ao Nang for the full week — one base, maximum relaxation. Everything is walkable or a short longtail boat ride away.

Why Ao Nang? Flat and stroller-friendly, Indian restaurants available, family resorts with kids' pools and slides, departure point for all island boats. Railay Beach is 15 min by longtail. Quieter and cheaper than Phuket.

Flexibility is key in shoulder season.

Activity
Emerald Pool (Sa Morakot): natural jungle pool fed by warm springs — crystal-clear blue-green water in the middle of rainforest. Short boardwalk trail (500m, stroller-possible). Toddlers can wade in the shallow edges. Then: Klong Thom Hot Springs — natural warm water streams flowing through the forest floor, multiple pools at different temperatures. Perfect temp for babies (35–40°C). Bring towels and change of clothes. Skip Tiger Cave Temple (1,260 steps — absolutely not with this group). Return for nap + pool time.

The rain game plan

Daily rhythm

7–8am: Wake up, hotel breakfast. 8am–1pm: Golden window — beach, islands, sightseeing. 1–3pm: Lunch + nap (rain usually hits here). 3–5pm: Pool or hotel play area. 5–7pm: Evening walk, shopping, sunset. 7–8pm: Dinner.

If a full rain day hits

Hotel pool (works in warm rain — kids don't care). Ao Nang shopping. Thai cooking class. Thai massage marathon. Room service + movie day. One lazy rain day is a gift, not a problem.

Book now checklist

  • Flights BLR → Krabi via KL: AirAsia MOVE app. Book May 10 (depart early AM) + May 17 (depart midday). Look for FLY-THRU fares.
  • Hotel in Ao Nang: 3 rooms at a resort with kids' pool. Top picks: Krabi Tipa, Holiday Inn Ao Nang, Ananta Burin, Panan Krabi.
  • Visa on arrival: Nothing to pre-book. Carry passport photos (2/adult) + 10,000 THB equivalent per person as proof of funds.
  • Travel insurance: Mandatory for Thailand entry. Family plan covering all 9.
  • Pack: Reef-safe sunscreen SPF 50, baby carriers (not strollers for beaches), ear protection for longtail boats, light rain jackets, mosquito repellent, swim diapers.
  • SIM cards: Buy at Krabi airport (~300 THB for 8-day unlimited). Get 2.
  • Currency: Withdraw THB from Krabi airport ATMs. Most restaurants are cash-only.

Why this works

For the kids

Warm shallow beaches. Hotel pools with slides. Boat adventures. Walking across the sandbar at Tup Island. Jungle hot springs. Mango sticky rice every night.

For your parents

Flat, walkable Ao Nang. Indian restaurants available. ₹700 Thai massages. No strenuous hikes. Warm weather they'll love. Buddhist temples to visit.

For you and Nikitha

A proper international family holiday. Incredible food. Stunning scenery. "We actually did it — 9 people, another country, 3 babies." Budget works with room to spare.

The honest trade-off

Hot (31°C) and humid. Rain will happen. 6–7h flight with layover. But: real beach paradise, excellent infrastructure, low season = fewer crowds + better prices.

Two plans, one decision. Himachal for the cool mountain magic. Krabi for the tropical beach dream. Both work. You choose.
