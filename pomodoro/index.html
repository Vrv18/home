Native Performance

Rewritten in Rust & Tauri. Under 10MB memory usage. Instant startup. It respects your system resources as much as it respects your time.

The Sanctuary

When focus ends, a full-screen restorative experience blooms. A digital temple to breathe, reset, and prepare for the next cycle.

Calm Mode

Feeling overwhelmed? Enter Calm Mode instantly from the menu. No timer, no pressure. Just a button that says "Breathe".

How to Use

The app lives quietly in your menu bar. There is no main window until you need it.

  • Left Click the menu bar icon to Start / Pause / Resume the timer.
  • Right Click to open the menu for options:
    • Adjust focus duration (25m, 45m, etc)
    • Enter Calm Mode immediately
    • Forfeit the current session
  • The Sanctuary appears automatically when a focus session completes. Breathe with the lotus. Click anywhere to close it.
